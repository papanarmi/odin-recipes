Beef Bulgogi

Description

Beef Bulgogi is a traditional Korean dish renowned for its tender, marinated beef slices grilled to perfection. The name "Bulgogi" translates to "fire meat," reflecting its origins in grilling over an open flame. This dish features thinly sliced beef marinated in a flavorful blend of soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, and other seasonings, resulting in a savory and slightly sweet taste profile. Often served with a side of rice and accompanied by various banchan (side dishes), Beef Bulgogi offers a delicious and satisfying dining experience cherished by many.

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced beef
  • Rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger and Garlic (minced)
  • Brown sugar
  • Green onions (for garnish)
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)
  • Korean chili paste (gochujang)

Steps

  1. Prepare Marinade
  2. Marinate Beef
  3. Preheat Grill or Pan
  4. Cook Beef
  5. Garnish and Serve on Rice
