Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish originating from the Indian subcontinent. It's characterized by its vibrant colors, aromatic spices, and tender chicken pieces layered with basmati rice. This beloved dish offers a symphony of flavors, blending spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and saffron with succulent chicken and fragrant rice. Often served with accompaniments like raita (yogurt sauce) and spicy pickles, Chicken Biryani is a celebratory dish enjoyed on special occasions and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (preferably bone-in, such as thighs or drumsticks)
  • Basmati rice
  • Onion (sliced)
  • Tomato (chopped)
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani masala
  • Saffron strands (soaked in warm milk)
  • Fresh cilantro and mint leaves (chopped, for garnish)
  • Ghee or cooking oil

Steps

  1. Marinate Chicken
  2. Prepare and Cook Rice
  3. Layer Ingredients
  4. Layer Rice and Saffron
  5. Cook Biryani
  6. Garnish and Serve
